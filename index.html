Hatsune Mik-who?

Hatsune Miku is a vocaloid (a computer-developed voice) developed by Crypton Future Media. Her name comes from the Japanese for first (初, hatsu), sound (音, ne), and future (ミク, miku), roughly translating to "the first sound of the future".
